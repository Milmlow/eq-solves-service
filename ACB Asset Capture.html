AH Amp Frame
What it is
The breaker's amp rating (frame size). Just the number.
Where to find it
Written on the breaker. Also: the number after 'NW' × 100 — e.g. NW16 = 1600A.
Example
1600
Site note
Quick check (Schneider Masterpact only — others read off the nameplate): NW08 = 800A, NW10 = 1000A, NW12 = 1250A, NW16 = 1600A, NW20 = 2000A, NW25 = 2500A, NW32 = 3200A, NW40 = 4000A.
